Welcome to the Sonata Archives!


This project serves a knowledge repository for formal music theory analyses of sonatas (and other classical pieces in dialogue with "Sonata Form").

It was created in 2018 by Hendrik Kits van Heyningen, inspired by work in the field of "Sonata Theory" by Hepokoski, Darcy and Monahan.
